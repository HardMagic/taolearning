TAO LEARNING

A WildSchool
to study Magic

  • Study with Us

It takes a village to raise a child
yet only one mentor to create a prodigy.

We work with a handful of children each year to help them explore their dreams where we inspire them to become geniuses, wizards, and mad scientists. We provide the atmosphere for them to discover themselves and encourage their passions wherever they may lie.
Our learning center nourishes these little leaders by getting out of their way and also setting strong examples in the community as to what success looks like. Whether your child wants to dance, write a novel, be a game developer, or conduct international business - Tao Learning is a life path suited for excellence and designed for the master within. We see a child's spirit age and spirit animal and speak to their spirit within. While some are 3,000 and others are 5, we give a guiding hand for children of all ages.
Headquartered in lovely Marietta, Ohio

Tao Learning Programs

Cottage Schooling

Your children may join us at our cottage in Marietta where we have many self-study areas, mentors, and classes held daily. We offer a cozy home atmosphere that encourages productivity.

Tao Home Schooling

We offer an online program your children can do on their own at home and join us in online discussions. A great option for those self motivated or living abroad.

Marietta Apprenticeship

Become a Tao Teacher and adopt our philosphy and lifestyle. Whether you want to open a Tao Learning Center or simply want your children to have the best education possible, learn our secret potions and style as an apprentice.

Virtual Apprenticeship

Discuss pedogagy and mentorship of future leaders in our online apprenticeship program. Here, you will put together study material and interact with our staff to awaken yourself to new teaching methods.
